About us

We started Dahomey Homestore in 2019 because as design enthusiasts making a home in Brussels, we struggled to find affordable and locally accessible furniture and homewares that truly reflected our own style and character.

Gallery

The Dahomey Homestore Gallery is adjacent to the Lois Lambert Gallery and the Gallery of Functional Art which has been in business for 33 years in Santa Monica, California at the Bergamot Station. Our Gallery Store carries artist-made and high-design work from all around the world.
